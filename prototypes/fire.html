Procedural flame — prototype

Multi-tongue flame shader (domain-warped fBm → blackbody ramp). Same GLSL that ports into the in-game fire.js. Torch/candle = one coherent tongue; bonfire = a broad, chaotic bed of tongues.

Embers, smoke and a flickering point-light are separate layers added in-game (this is the flame body). Drag a slider, or tell me "broader / wilder / lower" and I'll move the preset defaults.
