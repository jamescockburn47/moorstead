Carol — real MIDI through a sampled instrument

Public-domain carol MIDI arrangements (full harmony, correct tunes) played through real recorded instrument samples. No hand-synthesis. Pick a carol and a voice, hit Play.

Instruments are GM sample sets (MusyngKite, via the gleitz CDN — free); the MIDI is public-domain (Good King Wenceslas from the Timeless Truths hymn library; the rest from mfiles). The in-game build loads one chosen instrument + the MIDI set once, fades by village proximity, and rotates day-seeded. First Play of each instrument downloads its samples — give it a second.
